# Synthetic SAP Excerpt

Study SYN-001 is a randomized phase 2 oncology study.

Primary endpoint: Objective response rate using investigator assessment.

Analysis population: Efficacy population includes all randomized participants with measurable disease at baseline.

Multiplicity: No formal multiplicity adjustment is planned for subgroup analyses. Subgroup analyses are exploratory and descriptive.

P-value display rule: Report p-values to three decimal places. If p < 0.001, display as p < 0.001. Do not report p = 0.000.

Percentage display rule: Percentages are calculated as 100*n/N and displayed to one decimal place.

Safety population: All participants who received at least one dose of study treatment.
