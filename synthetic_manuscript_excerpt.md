# Synthetic Manuscript Excerpt

Study SYN-001 was a randomized phase 2 oncology trial comparing Drug A with control in adults with advanced solid tumors. A total of 120 participants were randomized 1:1 and 120 were included in the efficacy analysis.

## Abstract results

The objective response rate was 45/60 (76.0%) with Drug A and 30/60 (50.0%) with control (p = 0.000). Median progression-free survival was improved with Drug A, with a hazard ratio of 0.78 (95% CI, 0.61 to 0.99). In the female subgroup, Drug A demonstrated a significant benefit. No safety differences were observed between treatment arms.

## Results

The primary endpoint was investigator-assessed ORR. Drug A demonstrated superior efficacy versus control. Median follow-up was 8.1 months. Any treatment-emergent adverse event occurred in 48/60 (80.0%) participants receiving Drug A and 40/60 (66.7%) participants receiving control.

## Conclusion

Drug A provides a clinically meaningful and statistically proven benefit across subgroups with no safety difference compared with control.
